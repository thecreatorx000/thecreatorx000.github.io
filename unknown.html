Felix dies natales tibi, Astrake, sit;
Bonus et beatus sit,
cum multis amicis et multis donis
et cum multis felicitatibus.
Sic Catullus dixit, vivamus et amemus,
propterea quod vita brevis est!
May you have a good birthday, Astraki;
may it be good and blessed,
with many friends and many gifts,
and with much happiness.
As Catullus said: let us live and let us love,
since life is short!
